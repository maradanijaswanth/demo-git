Song lyrics are the words that make up a song, usually structured with verses and choruses, often written by a lyricist. They are closely linked to melodic rhythm and can be crafted by starting with a title, defining a theme, and choosing a structure. Tools like Shazam or Google's "hum to search" can help identify existing lyrics.
